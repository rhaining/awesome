Presidential Hopefuls

A brand new free screensaver for the Mac.

Download the screensaver (v1.0)

Presidential Hopefuls is a screensaver that visualizes polling data aggregated by FiveThirtyEight.

It is probably the best way to keep up with the race!

Based on a proprietary algorithm, the screensaver shows presidential candidates' faces ranked based on recent polls.
